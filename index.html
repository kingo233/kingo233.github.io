I. Powerful Numbers

Volodya always has a passion for math and numbers. And it seems to him that a integer number is powerful only when it is positive and divisible by each of its nonzero digits. He is eagerly to count the quantity of the powerful numbers in the given interval a,b. Can you help him?

Input Specification:

The first line contains one integer n (1≤n≤10) which is the number of queries. Then n lines follow, each line contains two integers a and b (1≤a≤b≤$9∗10^{18}$)

Output Specification:

Your answer should contains t numbers - answers to the queries, one number per line which is the number of powerful numbers for each given interval [a, b].

Sample Input:

Sample Output:

题意分析：

设一个数的各个数位为abcde….求满足abcdefg % lcm(a,b,c,d,e,f,g) == 0在指定区间内的数目

这道题是个数位dp,所谓数位dp，就是借助dfs和记忆化（可能还有剪枝操作）来完成对某种性质的数的计数。这道题用了很多技巧，如离散化节约空间，对2520的取模节约空间，需要一定的思维。

Sample Output:

题意分析：

大意是寻找在区间[a，b]内能被自身每一个非零数位整除的数字个数m。可以转而思考强力数字是自己各个位数上的数字的公倍数，而lcm（1，2，3，4，5，6，7，8，9）=2520， 因此强力数字各位的lcm会是2520的一个因子。因此存储数字的时候就可以存储$num$%2520来减少空间消耗，同时九个数字任意组合的lcm共有48个（程序求得），因此使用$dp$$[数位][截止当前数位的lcm][当前数mod2520]$来存储状态。

Prince xqly from MGIT falls in love with Princess lcy of the ICPC Kingdom, but Queen zxy of the ICPC Kingdom does not assent to this marriage.

Queen zxy doubts their love and advocates a task for Prince xqly as the premise for earning their happiness. For the lack of capacity, Prince xqly turns to you for help.

Here is the task: Princess lcy, King wjh, Queen zxy, ministers, maids, chefs, and many others are all together engaged in the task, all staying in separate rooms. Note that there is no empty room. Each one of them knows where he/she is and where any other people are.

Prince xqly is asked to find the princess. He can inquire anyone about the following three types of questions:

  • Who are you?
  • Who is staying in a specified room?

They will never refuse to answer the questions, but may not tell the truth. People, including Princess lcy herself, who support this marriage will present the facts. The opposition, like the queen, will always provide an incorrect answer. Other participants will be arbitrary.

B. Shooting

The girl Kaguya is standing in a bar and starring at Miyuki. She wants to shoot Miyuki with her heart arrow but she needs to know the distance between them to make her shot accurate.

The problem is that she wrote it in scientific notation. The scientific notation of some real number x is the notation of form AeB, where A is a real number and B is an integer and x=A∗10B is true. In our case A is between 0 and 9 and B is non-negative.

So she asked you to tell her the distance value in usual decimal representation with minimal number of digits after the decimal point (and no decimal point if it is an integer). See the output format for better understanding.

E. Kaguya’s Birthday Party

Kaguya is going to celebrate her birthday in her mansion. She wants to invite her friends to the birthday party.

排列计算 by江楠

发表于 2020-05-16 | | 阅读次数:

题目描述

天才程序员菜哭武和石头组队参加一个叫做国际排列计算竞赛 (International Competition of Permutation Calculation, ICPC) 的比赛，这个比赛的规则是这样的：

一个选手给出一个长度为 n 的排列，另一个选手给出 m 个询问，每次询问是一个形如 (l, r) 的数对，查询队友给出的排列中第 l 个数到第 r 个数的和，并将查询到的这个区间和加入总分，最后总分最高的队伍就能获胜。

石头手速很快，在比赛一开始就给出了 m 个询问；菜哭武也很强，他总是能找到最合适的排列，使得他们队的总分尽可能高。

在看比赛直播的你看到了石头给出的 m 个询问，聪明的你能不能预测出他们队伍最终的得分呢？

一个排列是一个长度为 n 的数列，其中 1 ~ n 中的每个数都在数列中恰好出现一次。比如 [1, 3, 2] 是一个排列，而 [2, 1, 4] 和 [1, 2, 3, 3] 不是排列。
